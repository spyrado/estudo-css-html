--- am / is / are ---

	is -> é -> singular
	are -> são -> plural
	am -> sou -> singular

-- SHORT FORM --

	i AM (I'am)
	he IS (he's)
	she IS (she's)
	it IS (it's)

--- VERBO TO BE(ser, estar) ---

	I am
	You are
	He/She/It is
	We are
	You are
	They are

--- Artigos Indefinidos ---
	A -> um/uma
	AN -> um/uma -> esse em específico é utilizado quando a outra palavra comecar com uma vogal.
